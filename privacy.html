Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Iftekhar LLC ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application, "3D Print Farm Simulator" (the "App"), available on the Google Play Store for Android devices. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not manually collect, request, or store any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or contacts. The App does not require you to create an account or log in.

2. Data Collected Automatically by Third Parties (AdMob)

The App is strictly ad-supported and uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. There are no In-App Purchases (IAP). While we do not collect data directly, the Google Mobile Ads SDK automatically collects certain information to provide and improve their services, and for advertising and marketing purposes. This automatically collected data includes:

  • Device Identifiers: Including your Android Advertising ID (AAID).
  • Network Information: Such as your IP address.
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on your network or device settings.
  • App Activity: Interactions with ads and the application.

3. How Information is Used

The data collected by Google AdMob is used exclusively by Google and its partners to:

  • Serve personalized and non-personalized advertisements within the App.
  • Analyze ad performance and user interaction.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of their advertising network.

Google uses cookies and mobile device identifiers (such as the AAID) to track your interests and deliver relevant ads. For more detailed information on how Google handles your data, please review the Google Privacy Policy and Google's guide on How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

4. Data Retention and Storage

Iftekhar LLC does not store any of your personal data on our servers. All data collected by third-party advertising services is stored, retained, and handled by Google in accordance with their respective privacy policies and retention schedules.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Our App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information. Additionally, we use settings configuration for AdMob to comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), which restricts personalized advertising for users known to be children.

6. California Resident Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have certain rights regarding your personal information under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). This includes the right to know what personal data is collected and the right to request deletion of your personal data. You also have the right to opt-out of the "sale" of your personal information. While we do not "sell" your data directly in the traditional sense, sharing identifiers with AdMob for personalized ads may be considered a "sale" under the CCPA. You can manage your ad preferences or opt-out of personalized ads directly through your Android device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

7. EU User Rights (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users in the European Economic Area (EEA) have the right to access, rectify, or erase their personal data, as well as the right to restrict or object to certain processing. We rely on your explicit consent to serve personalized ads. If you are in the EEA or the UK, you will be presented with a European regulations consent message upon opening the App for the first time, allowing you to choose whether to receive personalized or non-personalized ads. You can revoke or change this consent at any time.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
