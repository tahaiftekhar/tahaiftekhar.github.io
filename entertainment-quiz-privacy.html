Entertainment Quiz — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 19, 2026

Entertainment Quiz ("the app") is a trivia quiz app for Android about movies, TV shows and video games, developed by Taha Iftekhar / IFTEKHAR LLC ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Data the app sends to our servers

The app has no accounts and no sign-in. To provide quizzes, the app sends a small amount of data to our server:

  • Search queries — the title you type (e.g. "Friends") is sent to our server to look up matching movies, TV shows and games.
  • Quiz requests — the title you pick is sent so the matching quiz can be returned or generated.
  • Question reports — if you flag a question as wrong or broken, the question reference and the optional comment you type are sent to us so we can fix it.

These requests are not linked to your identity: we do not collect names, email addresses, precise location, or device identifiers on our servers, and we do not use them to build user profiles. Quiz content is generated with the help of AI language models; the title you request is shared with our AI provider (OpenAI) solely to create the quiz. Title search is powered by Wikidata and IGDB.

Advertising

The app shows ads served by Google AdMob. Like most ad-supported apps, AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain data to serve and measure ads, including your device's advertising identifier, IP address, coarse location, and diagnostic information. This collection is governed by Google's policies, not ours. See how Google uses data from apps that use its services and AdMob & data protection.

In the European Economic Area and the UK, the app shows a consent dialog before any ads are requested, and you can change your choice at any time from the app.

Data we do not collect

We do not collect contacts, photos, files, precise location, health data, or any personal identifiers. Your quiz scores stay on your device.

Children

The app is designed for a general audience and is not directed at children under 13.

Data retention and deletion

Search and quiz requests are processed to serve your request and may appear in short-lived operational logs. Question reports are kept until the reported question is reviewed. Because none of this data is linked to you, it cannot be looked up or deleted per person. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
