PrintCost IQ — Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 16, 2026

PrintCost IQ ("the app") is a 3D-print job quoting tool for Android, developed by Taha Iftekhar / IFTEKHAR LLC ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the app handles and how.

Data the app stores

PrintCost IQ is a backend-free app. Everything you enter — job details, quotes, printer presets, business profile (including an optional logo), and settings — is stored only on your device. We do not operate servers, we do not require an account, and we never receive, collect, or see your quotes or business data.

Data leaves your device only when you explicitly choose to export it: printing or sharing a PDF invoice, or creating a backup file. Those files go where you send them and are under your control. Uninstalling the app deletes its locally stored data.

Advertising

The app shows ads served by Google AdMob. Like most ad-supported apps, AdMob and its partners may collect and process certain data to serve and measure ads, including your device's advertising identifier, IP address, coarse location, and diagnostic information. This collection is governed by Google's policies, not ours:

  • How Google uses data from apps that use its services
  • Google Privacy Policy

In the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, the app asks for your consent before any ad-related data processing, using Google's consent framework. You can change your choice at any time via the "Ad privacy options" button inside the app. Declining consent does not remove any app functionality.

You can also reset or delete your device's advertising ID in Android's Settings → Privacy → Ads.

Permissions

The app requests network access solely for serving ads. No other sensitive permissions (contacts, location, camera, microphone, etc.) are requested.

Children

PrintCost IQ is a business tool and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
